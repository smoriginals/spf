Welcome to SP Foundations

We Are Committed To Making A Difference

SP Foundations is a non-profit organization dedicated to environmental protection, community development, and social welfare across India. Since our founding, we have been working tirelessly to create sustainable change.

Our mission is to empower communities through environmental action, education, and support programs, ensuring a better tomorrow for generations to come.

  • Dedicated to tree plantation and environmental awareness
  • Community support programs across 120+ villages
  • Clean water initiatives for rural populations
  • Food, shelter & disaster relief programs
  • Transparent and accountable donation process

Why Choose Us

Reason For Choose Us

We work across multiple areas to create lasting positive change in communities and the environment.

Tree Plantation

We have planted over 50,000 trees across India, contributing to reforestation and combating climate change one tree at a time.

Environmental Awareness

We conduct workshops, campaigns, and educational programs to spread awareness about protecting our environment.

Community Support

Our community support programs cover healthcare, education, and livelihood support for underprivileged families.

Clean Water Initiatives

We install water purification systems and dig wells to provide clean, safe drinking water to rural communities.

Food & Shelter

We provide food aid and temporary shelter to families affected by poverty, natural disasters, and displacement.

Disaster Relief

Our rapid response team provides immediate relief, supplies, and support to communities devastated by natural disasters.
