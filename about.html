Our Story

About SP Foundations

SP Foundations was established with a singular mission: to create sustainable positive change in communities across India through environmental action, social welfare, and community empowerment.

Founded by a group of passionate environmentalists and social workers, SP Foundations has grown from a small community initiative into a nationwide movement with hundreds of dedicated volunteers and donors.

Over the past decade, we have touched the lives of over 15,000 people across 120+ villages, planted 50,000+ trees, and provided clean water, food, and shelter to thousands of families in need.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To empower communities through environmental conservation, sustainable development, and social welfare programs that create lasting, measurable impact across rural and urban India.

Our Vision

A world where every community has access to clean water, food security, environmental sustainability, and equal opportunities — where no child goes to sleep hungry and no forest is lost to negligence.

Our Values

Integrity, transparency, inclusivity, and compassion guide everything we do. We believe in community-led solutions, sustainable approaches, and the power of collective action.

Our Programs

What We Do

Our comprehensive programs address multiple aspects of community development and environmental protection.

Tree Plantation

Organizing large-scale tree plantation drives across India — targeting 1 lakh trees by 2025. We focus on native species to restore biodiversity.

Clean Water

Installing water purification systems, digging wells, and educating communities about water conservation and sanitation practices.

Education Support

Providing scholarships, school supplies, and educational infrastructure to children in underserved communities across rural India.

Healthcare Camps

Organizing free medical camps with qualified doctors to provide healthcare services to remote villages with limited access to medical facilities.

Food Distribution

Regular food distribution drives ensuring no family goes hungry. We provide nutritious meals to thousands of underprivileged people monthly.

Disaster Relief

Rapid deployment of relief teams during floods, earthquakes, and other natural disasters to provide immediate assistance to affected populations.
